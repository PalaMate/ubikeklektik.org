Guiding Principles

We are evolving this universe following three guiding principles.

Small-scale community

Based on our past experience, we firmly believe in limited scale festivals These events create an intimate atmosphere,¬†in which each and every individual can easily add something to the whole.

Cavalcade of arts

We intend to bridge the gap between underground artists and genres that are presumably hard to attach to each other.

We are giving exposure to up and coming, eccentric projects. We are providing a radically free space for self-expression. The objective of UbikEklektik is to expand minds by the creation of a collective experience with the help of all the artists and participants. We are convinced that in a festival state of mind, the exposure to arts is amazingly grateful.

Sci-fi theme

Ubik is the ever changing universe where UbikEklektik takes place. The emphasis is on creating our own cosmos based on the participants ideas, and we welcome everyone to take a role and live with its narrative. Last year we introduced two tribes, whom this year will be joined by two new cultures, wanderers of our universes.
